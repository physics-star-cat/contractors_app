# EstiMate Promotion Strategy

## Overview

Paid niche apps have poor App Store discoverability. External traffic and social proof are required to drive downloads.

## Core Message

**Hook:** "Stop underquoting jobs"

Focus content on the problem, not the app:
- "I quoted $8k, job cost me $11k" - relatable pain
- "Why your estimates are always wrong" - educational hook
- Show the bell curve visualization - visually interesting and differentiating

## Social Media Strategy

### Target Platforms

| Platform | Why |
|----------|-----|
| Facebook | Contractor groups are very active |
| Instagram Reels | Short demos, visual content |
| TikTok | "Tradie-tok" audience in AU/NZ/UK |
| YouTube Shorts | Same content repurposed |

### Content Ideas

- Before/after: naive estimate vs P80 recommendation
- Quick demo of adding line items and running simulation
- "This math saved me $2k on my last job"
- Screen recording of PDF export for a real-looking quote

## YouTube Partnerships

### Target Channels

- Small/medium contractor channels (5k-100k subscribers)
- Trade-specific: electricians, plumbers, builders, HVAC
- Business-focused trade content (quoting, pricing, running a business)

Search terms to find channels:
- "contractor business tips"
- "how to quote jobs"
- "tradie business"
- "contractor pricing"

### Outreach Template

> Hey [name], love your content on [specific video]. I built an app that helps contractors avoid underquoting using probability math. Would you be interested in trying it free? If you like it, maybe we could do a quick mention or demo. Happy to discuss a sponsored spot too.

### What to Offer

- Free app access
- Promo code for their audience
- Script/talking points if they want them
- Screen recording they can use
- Sponsored spot fee ($100-500 for 20-50k sub channels)

### Tips

- Start with smaller channels (5k-50k) - more likely to respond
- Offer value first before asking for promotion
- Make it easy for them - provide assets
- Personalize outreach - reference specific videos

## Reviews Strategy

Social proof is critical for App Store conversion. Target: 5-10 genuine reviews.

- Give app free to contractor contacts in exchange for honest review
- Follow up with YouTube partners for reviews
- Respond to all reviews professionally

## Future Considerations

- Freemium model: basic calculator free, Monte Carlo simulation paid
- Localized App Store Optimization (keywords for UK/AU/NZ markets)
- Temporary price drop for launch momentum
